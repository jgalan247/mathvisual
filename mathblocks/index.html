SOHCAHTOA — The Three Ratios
In a right-angled triangle, the three trigonometric ratios connect an angle to the lengths of two sides. The mnemonic SOH-CAH-TOA helps you remember which ratio to use.
SOH — Sine
Use when you have or need the Opposite and Hypotenuse.
CAH — Cosine
Use when you have or need the Adjacent and Hypotenuse.
TOA — Tangent
Use when you have or need the Opposite and Adjacent.
How to use:
  1. Label the sides relative to the angle θ — which is opposite, adjacent, and the hypotenuse?
  2. Choose the ratio that connects the side you know with the side you need.
  3. Substitute the known values into the formula.
  4. Rearrange and solve for the unknown.
